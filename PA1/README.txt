## Instruction to clean:

####Command: make clean
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
## Instruction to compile:

####Command: make all
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
## Instruction to run:

####Command: ./udpPingServer ./udpPingClient (run on different terminals)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
## Description:

#### Your client will send a simple ping message to a server, receive a 
#### corresponding pong message back from the server, and determine the 
#### delay between when the client sent the ping message and received 
#### the pong message. This delay is called the Round Trip Time (RTT).
